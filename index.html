About the Club

Hastings Roller Hockey has been part of Hawke’s Bay life since the 1950s. We run learn-to-skate sessions, junior grades and senior squads at our open-air Windsor Park rink.

  • • Sunday training: 2–3 pm (weather dependent)
  • • Location: Windsor Park Rink, Hastings
  • • New to quads? We’ll get you rolling in minutes.

Club Values

Community first, fair play always, and constant skill growth.

What is Rink Hockey?

Fast, non-contact hockey on quad skates with a round cork-core ball and curved sticks. Five-a-side with rolling subs on ~34×17 m rinks.
